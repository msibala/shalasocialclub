Who Are We?

Shala Social Club is a gathering for anyone searching for a sense of home— especially those navigating life between cultures. Founded by Filipino immigrants Trish & Mia, Shala is rooted in the warmth of Filipino hospitality and built on the belief that culture is best shared through food, art, storytelling, and community.

Mission

To create soul-nourishing spaces where people can celebrate culture, connect with others, and feel at home—through gatherings inspired by the welcoming spirit of Filipino traditions.

Vision

To become a cultural homebase for immigrants, creatives, and community builders— starting with Filipino heritage, and growing into a global celebration of belonging.

why shala events?

Loneliness Epidemic

LA and NYC are hotspots for loneliness, particularly among young professionals and transplants.

Seeking Community IRL

We’ve seen the rise of third spaces, run clubs, Timeleft, lectures on tap, and The Table—even niche lookalike contests—all signaling that people want intentional, offline community again.

Cultural Gap

But we’re not seeing many social clubs that highlight culture and heritage. Shala exists to fill that gap.
